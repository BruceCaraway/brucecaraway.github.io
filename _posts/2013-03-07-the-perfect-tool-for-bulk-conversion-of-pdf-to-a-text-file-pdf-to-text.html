Don't you just love when you find that perfect tool (software or other) that fits the job perfectly?!? Just had that experience with PDF to Text from SeaSky Software.
I had over a hundred press releases in PDF format that I needed to  quickly extract to text so that each could be converted to a stand-alone blog post.
PDF to Text isn't sophisticated, but it doesn't need to be - it does exactly what it says it does.  As Albert Einstein (supposedly) said:

Make everything as simple as possible, but not simpler.
