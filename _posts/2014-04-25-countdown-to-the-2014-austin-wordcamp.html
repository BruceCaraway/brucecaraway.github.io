Anticipation mixed with a little fear of sleeping through my 5 am alarm is making it difficult to go to sleep tonight. Almost feels like the night before Christmas even!
Even so, I'll be hitting the road before sunrise in order to make the 2.5 hour drive over to Austin for this year's WordCamp.

If you have a chance to make it to this weekend's WordCamp, get there - You will not regret it!
